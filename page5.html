CONSIDERATION

The Members of the Trust are eligible for the Benefits that may be considered as Charity to work for five Principles of the Trust in detailed here under :

HEALTH

For the Medical and Surgical Treatment except the case of Pregnancy.

EDUCATION

For the Upliftment of Education and Sports in all respect.

SERVICE

For the Relief and Rehabilitation due to Natural Calamities and Explosion of all type.

CULTURE

For the Assistance due to Culture and Creative activities in general.

CO-OPERATION

For the Sublime Services due to development of Organization by way of continuous active Membership of the Trust.

NOTIFICATION

1. The Identity Card must be Renewed within 30 days from the last Date of Validity with current Photograph(s) to enjoy the Benefits, Advantages & Privileges of the Trust.

2. The “Certificate /Card” shall be issued by the Trust subject to requisite Application by the Individual donor / contributor of the Trust.

3. The Membership shall be Terminated if the Members are Convicted of any Offence and/or Punished by any Court of Law.

4. The Trust reserve the Rights to Accept or Reject any Application without assigning any reason or ground.

5. The Rights are reserve to add, alter, modify or suspend any and or all conditions and obligation at any time with or without Notice in the interest of the Trust.
