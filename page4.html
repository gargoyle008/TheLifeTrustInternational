APPLICATION

Any Person may Apply for the BENEFICIARY to fill up a requisite APPLICATION with stamp sized Photographs along with the non-refundable Admission / Entry Fee of Rs. 100/- (One hundred) only with or without any CONTRIBUTION. Who has attained the age of 18 years as the same way, may apply for the MEMBERSHIP of the Trust. The DONATION of Rs. 1,000/- (One Thousand) only and above or introduced minimum Application 10 x 10 BENEFICIARIES for GENERAL Rs. 10,000/- (Ten thousand) only and above or Organizational minimum 10 GENERALS for EXECUTIVE and Rs. 1,00,000 (One Lac) only and above or Organizational minimum 5 EXECUTIVES for the LIFE MEMBER as CONTRIBUTION to the Trust respectively. After Acceptance of such Application by the Trust, he/she shall be entitled to enjoy the Benefits, Advantages and Privileges subject to Terms and Validity of the of the Identity Card issued by the Trust from time to time.

OBLIGATION

The IDENTITY CARD must be Renewed within 30 days from the last Date of Validity with two copies of stamp size current Photographs against yearly Renewal Fee of Rs. 100/- (One hundred) only for the BENEFICIARY and GENERAL, as the same Rs. 1,000/- (One thousand) only for EXECUTIVE MEMBER respectively to enjoy the Benefits, Advantages and Privileges to be bound by the Memorandum, Rules and Regulations of the Trust.
