CO-ORDINATION The Beneficiaries of the Trust are eligible for the Upliftment of mankind that may be considered with or without Contribution as Coordination in detailed here under: 1. For the Green Revolution as a part of Anti Pollution Environment Programme such as Agriculture, Horticulture, Floriculture, Plantation, Forestry, Farming etc. 2. For the Development work of Life such as Dairy, Fishery, Poultry, Piggery, Hatchery. Animal Husbandry etc. 3. For the Incentive Training of Life as Technical, Medical, Physical, Mental etc. 4. For the Benefit of Helpless Children, Ladies and senior most Gentleman as Rehabilitation, Subscription, Subsidy etc. 5. For the Upliftment of any Social Obligation, as Cooperation, Consideration, Contribution, Identification, Grant, Gift, Fees etc.

CONDITION 1. The Assistance for Helpless, Children, Ladies and senior mast Gentleman, Application must be recommended by any Govt. Authorities. For the Medical and Surgical Treatment must have been checked by a Medical Officer of any Govt. Hospital or admitted in any Health Centre recognized by the Trust. 2. The Assistance for Incentive Training of Technical, Medica1, Physical, Mental & Social etc. Student must be intelligent and needy family recommended by any Govt. Authorities. 3. The Assistance for upliftment of Social Obligation, Creative and Cultural Activities, Person must be attend on Explanation and/ or Audition arranged by the Trust. 4. The Assistance for the Green Revolution on the Earth and / or any Development work of Life, evidence must be recommended by any Local Govt. Authorities. 5. The Assistance shall be allowed subject to Terms and Validity of the renewal Identity Card issued by the Trust.
