an appeal

Dear Friends,

The Life is a Natural felicity for and on alive Relations within Relationship. And the Human Lifeis the greatest Creation of the Earth in our Galaxy. On the way, the Life is a Journey without Trace.

We the Mankind, go from one stage to another day by day. The variety of roles that we have to play from time to time throughout our Life. But, still our prime needs Health, Education, Service, Culture & Co-Operation to all the sexes for co-existence in our Social Life.

Let us hold our hands to form a new World. We shall Live together and Rise together with each other to raise our sonorous Voice —

We are the Son of this Universe,

We should Strong each Other

With the Message in our heart....

“LIVE AND LET LIVE”
